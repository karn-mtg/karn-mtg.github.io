# Introducing Karn Forge

We've been building in the dark for a while now, and today we're ready to share what we've been working on: **Karn Forge**, a free and open-source deck builder for Magic: The Gathering.

## Why Another Deck Builder?

The existing tools are either paywalled, browser-only, or lack the kind of visual canvas that makes deckbuilding feel creative rather than transactional. We wanted something that feels like a design tool — infinite canvas, drag-and-drop, sticky notes, the works.

The name comes from Karn, the silver golem and planeswalker — a builder and creator of worlds, which felt right.

## What's in v0.1

- **Visual infinite canvas** — see your whole deck at once, organized how you think
- **Card search** powered by Scryfall — every card, every printing, updated constantly
- **Deck export** in text, MTGO, and Arena formats
- **Offline first** — works fully without internet after initial sync
- **Windows and macOS** — both supported

## What's Next

We're working on:

- **Collection import** from Moxfield, Archidekt, Deckbox, and CSV
- **Price tracking** with historical charts
- **Analytics dashboard** — mana curve, type distribution, synergy maps
- **Deck sharing** — shareable links and QR codes
