# Supported Formats

Karn Forge supports deck building and legality checking for the following Magic: The Gathering formats. When you create a deck, you choose a format — the format is used for the **Missing** tab comparisons and displayed on the deck card.

## Formats

### Commander (EDH)

**Deck size:** 100 cards (including the commander)
**Copies allowed:** 1 of each card (except basic lands)
**Special rules:** Exactly 1 legendary creature or planeswalker designated as commander; deck must be within the commander's color identity; 40 life starting total in multiplayer.

Commander is the most popular casual format and has the broadest deck building latitude.

---

### Modern

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands)
**Card pool:** Sets from 8th Edition and Mirrodin (2003) onward, excluding banned cards.

A competitive non-rotating format with a large card pool.

---

### Standard

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands)
**Card pool:** Sets from the last two years (rotates annually in fall). Check the [official Standard rotation schedule](https://magic.wizards.com/en/formats/standard) for the current card pool.

The primary rotating format, used at most local game stores and major tournaments.

---

### Pioneer

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands)
**Card pool:** Sets from Return to Ravnica (October 2012) onward, excluding banned cards.

A non-rotating format between Standard and Modern in terms of card pool size.

---

### Legacy

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands)
**Card pool:** All cards ever printed (except the Reserve List and banned cards).

A high-power competitive format with access to the full Magic history.

---

### Vintage

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands and restricted cards, which are limited to 1)
**Card pool:** All cards ever printed except the small banned list.

The most powerful sanctioned format. Many strong cards are restricted to 1 copy rather than banned outright.

---

### Pauper

**Deck size:** 60 cards minimum (15-card sideboard optional)
**Copies allowed:** Up to 4 of each card (except basic lands)
**Card pool:** Cards that have been printed at common rarity in any Magic Online release, excluding banned cards.

A budget-friendly format limited to common rarity cards.

---

## Legality data

Format legality in Karn Forge comes from the Scryfall card database, maintained by the Arsenal service. Legality is checked per-card and displayed in the **Card Detail** panel.

<Aside type="note">
  Karn Forge does not enforce deck legality rules during deck building — it's a creative tool, not a validator. The legality display is informational. Always verify legality with the official format rules before playing in a sanctioned event.
</Aside>

## Custom formats

Karn Forge does not currently support custom format definitions (e.g., Cube, Brawl, Historic). You can build decks for these formats without selecting a format, or use any existing format as a loose approximation.
