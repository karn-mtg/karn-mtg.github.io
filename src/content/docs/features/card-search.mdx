# Card Search

The Card Search panel lets you search the full Scryfall card database — all cards, all printings — directly from within Karn Forge, without an internet connection (after the first Arsenal setup).

## Opening the search panel

The Card Search panel lives on the right side of the **Deck Workshop**. Open it with:

- `Ctrl+F` / `Cmd+F`
- The magnifier icon in the right sidebar

## Searching for cards

Type any part of a card name into the search box. Results appear as you type, ranked by relevance. The search is case-insensitive and matches partial names.

**Examples:**

| Query | Matches |
|---|---|
| `lightning` | Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning, Lightning Helix, … |
| `sol ring` | Sol Ring |
| `teferi` | All Teferi planeswalkers and creatures |

<Aside type="tip">
  The search returns up to 50 results. If you don't see what you're looking for, try a more specific query.
</Aside>

## Adding cards to your deck

From the search results, you can add a card to the deck in two ways:

- **Click the `+` button** on the result card to add one copy at the deck's center viewport position.
- **Drag the result card** onto the canvas to place it exactly where you want it.

Both actions add the card to the deck list and place it on the canvas simultaneously.

## Card detail panel

Click any card in the search results (or any card on the canvas) to open the **Card Detail** panel. It shows:

- **Full card image** — click the image to cycle through available printings
- **Mana cost and mana symbols** — rendered with official mana pip icons
- **Type line** — full type including supertypes, card types, and subtypes
- **Oracle text** — the authoritative rules text for the card
- **Power / Toughness / Loyalty** — for creatures and planeswalkers
- **Format legality** — a grid showing the card's legality in Commander, Modern, Standard, Pioneer, Legacy, Vintage, and Pauper
- **EDHREC rank** — how popular the card is in Commander decks on EDHREC
- **Price** — current market price data (requires network connection to update)

### Changing the printing

The detail panel shows the currently selected printing and an art thumbnail strip for alternate printings. Click any thumbnail to switch the art used on the canvas for this card.

<Aside type="note">
  Art selections are per-deck and per-arrangement. Two arrangements of the same deck can show different printings of the same card.
</Aside>

## Global Search

In addition to the in-deck Card Search panel, Karn Forge has a **Global Search** accessible from the top bar. Global Search searches across your decks and library by deck name, card name, or format.

Open Global Search with `Ctrl+K` / `Cmd+K`.
