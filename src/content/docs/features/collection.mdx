# Collection

The Collection page lets you track your physical Magic: The Gathering cards. You can record each card's condition, quantity, foil status, and market price — and assign cards to recipients for trade and order management.

## Adding a card to your collection

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to **Collection** in the left sidebar.
  2. Use the **Card Search** panel on the right to find a card.
  3. Click the card in the search results to select it.
  4. Fill in the entry form:
     - **Quantity** — how many copies
     - **Condition** — see the condition guide below
     - **Foil** — check if the copy is foil
     - **Price** — optional market price per copy
  5. Click **Add** to record the entry.
</Steps>

## Card condition grades

Karn Forge uses the standard TCGPlayer condition grades:

| Grade | Full name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **NM** | Near Mint | Card shows no visible wear. Corners are sharp, surface is clean. |
| **LP** | Lightly Played | Minor surface wear, slight edge wear. Sleeveable without concern. |
| **MP** | Moderately Played | Visible wear — creases, scuffs, or scratched corners. Still tournament legal. |
| **HP** | Heavily Played | Significant wear — deep creases, major edge wear, possible water damage. |
| **DMG** | Damaged | Severe damage — tears, writing, or structural issues. |

## Managing your collection

### Editing an entry

Click any row in the collection table to expand inline editing. You can change the quantity, condition, foil flag, price, and recipient without opening a separate dialog.

### Removing a card

Hover over a row and click the **Delete** icon that appears on the right.

### Sorting and filtering

Click any column header to sort the table by that column. Click again to reverse the sort order. Available sort columns:

- Name
- Quantity
- Condition
- Price
- Foil

Use the **Search** bar at the top of the app to filter the collection by card name in real time.

## Foil tracking

Each collection entry can be marked as foil. Foil entries are shown with a distinct badge in the table. Foil status is tracked separately from condition and quantity — you can have both NM foil and NM non-foil copies of the same card as separate entries.

## Price tracking

You can manually enter a price per copy for each collection entry. Prices are stored locally and are not automatically updated. To update prices, edit the entry and enter the current market value.

<Aside type="tip">
  Prices are shown in the collection table's total column as `quantity × price per copy`. Use this to estimate the total value of your collection at a glance.
</Aside>

## Recipients

Recipients let you assign collection cards to specific people — useful for managing trades, sales, or organized group orders.

### Creating a recipient

<Steps>
  1. Click **Manage Recipients** in the collection header.
  2. Click **New Recipient** and enter a name.
  3. Save.
</Steps>

### Assigning a card to a recipient

Edit a collection entry and select a recipient from the **Recipient** dropdown. The card row will show the recipient's name, making it easy to see which cards are spoken for.

## Deck integration

The **Missing** tab in the **Deck Workshop** compares your deck list against your collection and shows every card you don't own (or don't have enough copies of). Use this to build a shopping list for a deck.
