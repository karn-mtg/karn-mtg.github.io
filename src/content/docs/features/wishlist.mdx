# Wishlist

The Wishlist is a prioritized list of cards you want to acquire. It's separate from your [Collection](/features/collection) (cards you own) and works as a shopping and planning tool.

## Adding a card to your wishlist

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to **Wishlist** in the left sidebar.
  2. Use the **Card Search** panel on the right to find a card.
  3. Click the card in the results to select it.
  4. Set the **Quantity** and **Priority**.
  5. Click **Add to Wishlist**.
</Steps>

## Priority levels

Each wishlist entry has a priority level:

| Priority | Use case |
|---|---|
| **Low** | Nice-to-have upgrades; no rush |
| **Normal** | Cards you'll pick up when convenient |
| **High** | Cards you actively need soon |
| **Critical** | Cards blocking a deck from being played |

Priority is shown with color coding in the table so the most important cards stand out.

## Reordering entries

Drag any row by its drag handle on the left to reorder the wishlist. Your custom order is saved automatically in local storage.

<Aside type="tip">
  Sort your wishlist by priority first, then drag within priority groups to set your preferred acquisition order.
</Aside>

## Adding a wishlist card directly to a deck

Each row has an **Add to Deck** button. Click it to open a deck picker, then click any deck to add the card to that deck's list. The card is added with a quantity of 1.

This is useful when you've decided a wishlist card belongs in a specific deck and want to track it there as a missing card.

## Removing a card

Hover a row and click the **Remove** icon on the right, or select multiple rows and use the bulk delete option.

## Wishlist vs Collection

| | Collection | Wishlist |
|---|---|---|
| Purpose | Cards you own | Cards you want |
| Tracks condition | Yes | No |
| Tracks price paid | Yes | No |
| Tracks priority | No | Yes |
| Works with deck Missing tab | Yes (cards you have) | No |
